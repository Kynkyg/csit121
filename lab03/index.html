Burmese Egg Curry

Recipe created by John Hein

Ingredients needed

  • 3 large white eggs
  • 1 whole tomatoes
  • 1/2 yellow onions
  • 1/4 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 2 red chilies

Preparing the ingredients:

  1. Boil some eggs while we prep the rest of the ingredients
  2. Once the eggs are boiling, chop tomatoes and onions
  3. Blend the tomatoes, chilies, and onions all together
  4. After blended, put into a hot pan and cook the tomatoes down, and season with chicken bouillon
  5. Cut boiled eggs in half and gently place them in the pan
  6. Once eggs are perfectly coated in the tomato curry, serve with rice

This is my favorite recipe because it is an easy recipe that requires only 5 simple ingredients. It also does not take too long to make. My mom made it when I was a child, and it was the best. This is a very important dish in my childhood, and I still make it a lot today.
