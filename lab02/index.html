Burmese Egg curry

Recipe created by Jhon Hein

Ingredients needed

  • 3large white eggs
  • 1whole tomatoes
  • 1/2yellow onions
  • 1/4 tablespoonchicken bouillon
  • 2red chilies

Preparing the ingredients:

  1. Boil some eggs while we prep the rest of the ingredients
  2. once the eggs are boiling, chop tomatoes and onions
  3. Blend the Tomatoes, chilies, and onions all together
  4. After blended, put into a hot pan and cook the tomatoes down, and season with chicken bouillon
  5. Cut boiled eggs in hald and gently place them in pan
  6. Once eggs are perfectly coated in the tomatoe curry, serve with rice

    7. This is my favorte recipe because it is an easy recipe that requires only 5 simple ingredients. It also does not take too long to make. My mom made it when I was a child and it was the best. This is a very important dish in my childhood and I still make it a lot today.
