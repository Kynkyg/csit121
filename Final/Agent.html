Agent Name - Iso

Iso is a new agent in Valorant. Hailing from China, his real name is Li Zhao Yu. Widely known by his nickname, "The Dead Lilac," Iso was introduced to the game's roster on October 30, 2023.

His distinctive appearance includes an overall white-colored hoodie with dark purple accents. Iso sports a pair of eye-catching purple sneakers with red laces. Notably, he wears a bandage on his face and prefers to use earbuds during operations.

When it comes to Iso's personality. Iso possesses a laid-back yet confident demeanor, an air of mystery surrounding his early life adding an enigmatic touch to his character. He finds solace in the rhythm of music during combat. His arrogance occasionally surfaces, evident in his inclination toward one-on-one duels rather than engaging in larger skirmishes. Though he is arrogant, he is extremely helpful to his team with his wits.
